Decentralized Poll System

Welcome!

Welcome to Decentralized Polls, a platform where your voice truly matters! Here, we leverage the power of blockchain to create a secure, transparent, and tamper-proof voting system. Join us to express your opinion on various topics and contribute to a decentralized, community-driven decision-making process.

Whether you’re voting on social issues, entertainment, or trending debates, your input is recorded permanently on the blockchain, ensuring fairness and trust. Let’s reshape how polling works—together.

How It Works?

It’s simple! Browse through the list of polls available on the platform and cast your vote on the ones that interest you. Every vote is securely stored on the blockchain, making it immutable and accessible for transparent verification.

By using blockchain technology, we ensure that no single entity can manipulate the results. Your vote counts—accurately and securely—every time.

Log In with MetaMask

To participate, you’ll need to log in with MetaMask, your digital wallet. MetaMask allows us to verify your identity and securely link your vote to your blockchain address. Don’t worry—we don’t collect personal data; your privacy is our priority.
